Our History

2018

The desire to set up a quality burger restaurant began.

2019

Two friends with a passion for burgers decided to buy a food truck and create a simple but high quality menu.

2020

We started serving our delicious burgers at some festivals and the feedback from people was incredible. Although covid was a big financial problem but we didn't give up.

2021

We already had two food trucks and some festivals in Europe started to call us to serve food.

2022

We have finally decided to open our first restaurant in Valencia, Spain!!!! A small but cosy restaurant.

2023

We will open a second restaurant in Dublin because we love the city and because one of the partners is Irish!!!
